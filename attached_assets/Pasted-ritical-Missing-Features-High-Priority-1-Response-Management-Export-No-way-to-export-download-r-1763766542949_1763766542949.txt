ritical Missing Features (High Priority)
1. Response Management & Export
No way to export/download responses - Essential for any survey tool. Need CSV/Excel export, JSON export
No response filtering/search - Can't search by respondent or find specific responses
No bulk actions - Can't delete/archive multiple responses at once
Response deduplication - No detection of duplicate submissions (same IP, same answers, etc.)
2. Survey Control & Scheduling
No survey expiration/closure dates - Surveys stay open indefinitely
No response limits/quotas - Can't cap the number of responses
No survey scheduling - Can't set start/end dates or publish at specific times
No survey status (Active/Paused/Closed) - No way to pause and resume surveys
3. Advanced Question Logic
No skip logic/branching - All respondents see all questions; can't show questions conditionally
No question randomization - Question order is always the same (bias risk)
Limited question types - Need matrix tables, ranking, NPS, date pickers, file uploads
No question groups/sections - Can't organize questions into logical sections with titles
4. Response Quality & Validation
No response time tracking - Can't detect rushed responses (completed in 2 seconds)
No attention checks - No way to validate respondent attention
No required field validation feedback - Users might not know why they can't submit
No progress bar on questions - Respondents don't know how long survey is
No response timeout - Session can last forever
5. Analytics & Insights (Currently listed but likely minimal)
No data visualization dashboard - Just collecting responses, not showing trends
No comparison across surveys - Can't benchmark question A vs question B
No sentiment analysis - Open text responses aren't analyzed
No response rate metrics - No tracking of how many people started vs completed
No demographic breakdown - Can't segment responses by respondent characteristics
No real-time dashboard - No live response stream
6. Respondent Management
No respondent tracking/authentication - Anyone can respond multiple times
No respondent invitations - No email/link distribution system
No pre-population/SSO - Can't pre-fill known respondent data
No anonymous vs. identified modes - No choice between tracking respondents or staying anonymous
7. Survey Versioning & Collaboration
No survey versioning - Can't track changes or revert to previous versions
No team collaboration - Only individual survey creators
No comments/notes - No way to leave feedback on drafts
No approval workflow - All surveys go straight to publish
8. Integration & Distribution
No email integration - Can't send survey invitations via email
No webhook/API for external systems - Can't pipe responses to CRM, Slack, etc.
No embedded surveys - Can't embed on websites (iframe support)
No QR codes - Can't generate QR codes for physical distribution
9. Compliance & Security
No GDPR/privacy compliance (consent forms, data deletion)
No survey versioning for audit - Can't prove what version respondent saw
No role-based access control - No user roles (admin, viewer, editor)
No API keys/access control - No way to secure programmatic access
Nice-to-Have Features (Medium Priority)
Survey templates library - Pre-built templates for common use cases (NPS, feedback, training eval, etc.)
White-label option - Custom domain and branding removal
Mobile app - Native mobile experience (currently web-only)
Multilingual support - Surveys in multiple languages
Smart question recommendations - AI suggests follow-up questions based on responses
Response notifications - Real-time alerts when new responses arrive
Survey cloning - Duplicate surveys to create variations
Bulk respondent upload - Import email list for targeted surveys
Survey comparison - Run A/B test variations and compare results
Custom thank you pages - Redirect to custom URL after completion
Operational Best Practices Missing
Response Quality Assurance - No way to validate or clean data
Survey Versioning - No audit trail for compliance
Respondent Communication - No email system for invitations/reminders
Data Governance - No retention policies or deletion workflows
Performance Metrics - No SLA tracking, response time analysis
